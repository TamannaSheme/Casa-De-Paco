Signature Drinks

“Winner of the 2019 Newark Cocktail Competition”

Paco Luca Albariño (16)

Fresh passion fruit pulp, premium vodka, Malibu and passion nectar.

Ibiza Tea (15)

Our lemony take on the popular LIT; vodka, rum, gin, tequila triple sec, simple syrup and Kas Limon

Valenciana (16)

Cava, vodka, Gran Marnier and orange juice

Tonica-Bron (15)

Bringing Spain’s gin and tonic craze to your door step, Hendricks gin, muddled blueberries and limes, bitters, tonic and a splash of Kas Limon

Pantera Rosa (16)

Muddled blueberries, cucumber vodka, gin, St. Germaine and simple syrup

Paloma Borracha (16)

Muddled oranges, orange bitters, mezcal, pineapple juice, simple syrup, splash of Spanish brandy and cinnamon

Manhattan (16)

Bourbon, sweet vermouth, Campari, orange pieces and blood orange bitters

Cask Aged Cocktails aged for over a month in oak barrels, best served over ice
